I’ve been involved in a project at my church called 100 Day Bible Challenge. We’re currently reading through 100 key parts of the Bible every day for 100 days and I am hoping to do a bit of daily artwork inspired by what we’ve read. Its been great so far- we have 360 people on the Facebook group and loads of discussions going on. Check the website for more details.
