One of the most rewarding and well-used projects I've worked on at Yoomee, Elefriends is an online community for people who struggle with mental health issues. A closed, safe space for people to be real and to support others.

Like vInspired, we started this project as a small prototype and developed it gradually over time, testing, learning and making it better. I was involved with design, UX and front-end build.
