I have finally got around to updating my site, which is something that I’ve been intending to do for many years now.

After lots of complex ideas, I decided to keep things really simple; One page with some info about me, this blog, and an online sketchbook of drawings and ideas. It all plays nicely on mobiles too.

For the nerds, I used github pages for the homepage and the rest is just a customised tumblr theme. I decided to go with tumblr over Wordpress, as I’m a big fan of how easy it is to post from their apps, and love the sharing/social side. (Also was inspired by the Tumblr founder David Karp’s talk at sxsw this year).

I’d love to hear your feedback on the design (leave a comment below or send me a tweet), and I promise to keep this blog more updated than the last.
