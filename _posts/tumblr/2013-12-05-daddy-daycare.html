Last Summer I was given a great chance to start working four days a week so that I could spend time with a little bundle of energy called Sophie. As we embarked on our Daddy Day Care experiment my wife challenged me to try to do something different every week. The result has been recorded in Sophie’s inimitable style here.

Blogging about and living Tuesdays in this way has reminded me that the places we explore the least are often the places on our own doorstep. My adventures with Sophie have taken us to new parts of our city and the countryside, we’ve discovered giant spoons, burritos, the joys of Ikea and managed to fit in a fair bit of longboarding too!
