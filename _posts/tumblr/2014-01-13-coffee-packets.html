After collecting coffee packets for the last 5 years (not really sure why), I have decided to do something creative with them.

This is my first ever coffee packet typeface. I cut each one out by hand and covered my room with stale coffee grounds - it was a labour of love! It’s early days (there aren’t any numerals or punctuation yet), but you can download the images for free here. Make something! (I’d love to know what you come up with).
