Hello my name is Rich. I’m a designer, illustrator and maker of things.

  • I’m creative director at Yoomee. We build websites for charities and organisations who want to improve the world.
  • I spend lots of time drawing, taking photos, coding things and telling stories. My blog records what I get up to and what I’m working on.
  • On Tuesdays I take the day off to explore the world with my daughter Sophie. Each week we do something new and share it on our Daddy Daycare blog.
  • I like a challenge. A while back I created 100 illustrations over 100 days for a project I set up at my church. You can download all the images for free here.
