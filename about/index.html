I’m a designer who loves to make things, tell stories and solve problems.

  • I’m creative director at Yoomee. We build websites for charities and organisations who want to do good things.
  • I spend lots of my time drawing, and regularly post doodles, ideas and thoughts here. It's my sketchbook for the internet.
  • On Tuesdays I take the day off to explore the world with my daughter Sophie. We have a lot of fun, and this blog records what we get up to.
